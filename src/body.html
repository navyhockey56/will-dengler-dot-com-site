About Me

About me? For the most part, I'm your typical software engineer. My professional experience has been focused on backend systems, with a particular focus on integrations with third party services. If you're interested in more details about my career, then you should head on over to my LinkedIn. Professionally, I typically work with ruby and java; although, I much prefer ruby. Writing software is not just my job, but also happens to be one of my favorite things to do. As such, my free time is typically spend tinkering on some side project - most of which you can find on my Github.

Outside of software, I have a passion for mathematics, and I love to share this with others through my Youtube videos and website Awkward Math. When it comes to math, my interest veer towards abstract algebra, graph theory, and number theory. Like most math enthusiasts, I find prime numbers to be incredibly interesting and have dedicated countless hours of my life trying to understand them better. In my most recent expedition into understanding prime numbers, I created a state-based machine for prime number discovery which I have termed as "Awkward State Machines", you learn more about Awkward State Machines, and even visualize one running over on Awkward Math. Outside of the prime numbers, I love exploring mathematics that allows me to utilize my skills as a programmer. The natural compatibility between the two subjects has been a strong influence on what and how I study mathematical systems. During my senior year of college, the synergy of the two subjects led me to develop a method for producing three, four, and five dimensional graphs using space and color, which I have dubbed "Color Graphs". I had the honor of presenting my methods during the poster session of University of Maryland's 2017 February Fourier Talks prior to my graduation that spring. I have since obtained US Patent US20180293767A1 for the method. Today, anyone can freely create Color Graphs over on Awkward Math.

There's not much more to tell. Like most 20-something year olds, I love to go out with friends, and spend a bit too much time streaming re-runs of my favorite shows. Most of the time you'll find me in DC, feel free to drop me a message if you're in the area and want to grab a drink. For those of you that play, you can find me virtually on my Oldschool Runescape ironman character.
